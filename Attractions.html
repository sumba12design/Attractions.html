Top Attractions In Amboseli National Park

It’s known for its large elephant herds and views of immense Mount Kilimanjaro, across the border in Tanzania. Observation Hill offers panoramas of the peak and the park’s plains and swamps. Varied wildlife includes giraffes, zebras, cheetahs and hundreds of bird species. The western section is dominated by vast Lake Amboseli, which is dry outside the rainy season.

Animal life

There is a diverse collection of Animals in Amboseli and the park is much valued for its rich wildlife, particularly the majestic herds of elephant. There are about 80 species of Mammals and over 400 species of birds in Amboseli and the sorrounding private wildlife conservancies. Amboseli National Park is a special place, known for more than just its large numbers of elephant herd, prides of lion and leopard. You can also spot the so called ''Big 5'', namely Lion, Elephant, Leopard and Buffalo expect for Rhinos. lions in samburu

Maasai culture/Visiting local villages

When you get to the Masai villages, you are welcomed with the songs and dance from the locals and after that you are free to move around the villages, take to the locals, participate in some of the activities like dancing, milking the cows, interact with the locals and also take photos with them.

Elephants Research Camp

Amboseli Elephant research Camp is a great site offering the visitors of the park with the most amazing opportunity to learn more about elephants.The project was mainly established to ensure the long-term conservation and welfare of the Africa's elephants in the context of human needs and pressures through scientist research, training, community outreach, public awareness and advocacy.
